Placement Season Summary Report

Overview
A total of 142 companies visited campus during the last placement season,
spanning core engineering, IT services, product-based startups, and
analytics-focused recruiters. The season ran from August through April.

Package Statistics
The highest package offered was 44 LPA, extended by a product-based company
for a software engineering role. The average package across all offers was
approximately 7.8 LPA. The median package was 6.2 LPA.

Branch-wise Participation
Computer Science and AI/ML specialization students accounted for the largest
share of offers, followed by Electronics and Communication, and Information
Technology. Mechanical and Civil branches saw increased participation from
core infrastructure and manufacturing recruiters this season.

Role Distribution
Software development roles made up the largest category of offers, followed
by data analyst and business analyst roles, with a smaller number of roles
in core hardware, DevOps, and QA testing.

Internship-to-Offer Conversion
Of the students who completed a pre-placement internship, approximately 68%
converted their internship into a full-time offer at the same company.
