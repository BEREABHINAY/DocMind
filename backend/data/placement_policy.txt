Placement Eligibility Policy

Section 1: Academic Eligibility
Candidates must have a minimum aggregate of 60% across all semesters completed
so far, with no active backlogs at the time of registration for any placement
drive. Students who clear a backlog after registration but before the drive
date may still be considered, subject to approval from the placement cell.

Section 2: Branch Restrictions
Certain core companies restrict eligibility to specific branches such as CSE,
IT, ECE, and AI/ML specializations. Students should check the job description
posted on the placement portal for branch-wise eligibility before applying.

Section 3: Registration Requirements
Students must submit an updated resume, a copy of their academic transcript,
and a signed code-of-conduct declaration to complete registration for any
placement drive. Incomplete submissions will not be processed.

Section 4: Number of Attempts
A student may appear for a maximum of two on-campus drives per company per
academic year. Students who receive and accept an offer are moved to the
"placed" pool and become ineligible for further core-company drives, unless
the accepted offer falls under the dream-company exception listed in Section 5.

Section 5: Dream Company Exception
Students placed in a company below a package threshold of 6 LPA may still
sit for drives explicitly marked as "dream companies" later in the season.

Section 6: Code of Conduct
Any student found sharing interview questions in a way that violates a
company's confidentiality agreement, or found using unfair means during an
online assessment, will be barred from all future placement activity for
one academic year.
